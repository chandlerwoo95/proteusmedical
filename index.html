Storyview

Discover the moments behind all your favorite places in this geolocation, photo sharing app


Created by iOS Developer Phillip Ou at the YC Hackathon, Storyview transforms the photo sharing experience by integrating the excitement of travel with the fulfillment of storytelling. Each photo is saved in a specific location along with the rich story behind it--awaiting travelers to discover it when they visit. Utilizing Parse's geolocation backend, users see all the experiences--from the heartwarming to the humorous--that have occurred at each destination they visit.
By allowing more text to describe the moment, Storyview harkens back to a time when accounts of our adventures didn't merely travel through short tweets and quick snap shots. While certainly a departure from other photo sharing applications, Storyview's promotion of a more methodical, thoughtful photo sharing process will ultimately provide a richer, more fulfilling story telling experience.

Audity

Share an activity or mood and let other users recommend the music you like

Audity is a social music recommendation service that allows users to post a status of what they are doing and what music they want to listen to. Other users can then contribute songs to these dynamic playlists. While there are a number of applications that utilize algorithms to conduct music recommendations, I often find myself disregarding their suggestions all together and listening to what my friends recommend. After all, a recommendation from someone close to you bears much more significance than one generated by an algorithm. There are a number of outlets with the capability to share media, but no one true platform that focuses entirely on that experience. Audity seeks to provide that service to users and create a fun, music-loving community that are just as eager about sharing their songs as listening to them.

Formwear

Let fashion savvy users from around the world pick out your next wardrobe.

A lot of us often times struggle with the daunting task of picking out a good-looking outfit. Some slave away for hours deciding between combinations of clothes while others complacently just throw on the first thing they see. Cloth simplifies this painful process by allowing other users to recommend outfits best suited for the user's style, personality, and appearance. Users will then have review these suggestions and decide for themselves which selection is bested suited for them.
