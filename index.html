«About Me»

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Chandler Woo and I am a biomedical engineer from Pasadena, California. I am currently majoring in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Rochester in Upstate New York. Creating new experiences is how I have lived my life. You may find me backpacking in the backcountry of New Mexico, taking a polar plunge in the icy waters of Alaska, or even just exploring the local areas of New York. I have found a deep-rooted passion for medical devices, where I hope to enhance the lives of others through the wonders of engineering and science. Feel free to browse through the website and be sure to contact me if you have any further questions!
