Data Quantification with Statistical Accuracy

Rascal was the first ever mobile application I ever wrote. It was a truly wonderful experience learning the Objective-C language from scratch and seeing all the positive feedback after its release. Looking back, it's a pretty simple application, but it's particularly important because it showed me the value in building things that bring joy to others

Get rewarded for embarrassing your loved ones! Set a bounty on one of your friends, wait for people to snap a funny photo, and reap the rewards. Rascal accumulates your photos in your Portfolio and posts your best ones in the Gallery. Be responsible, but also remember that some times the best way to laugh is at the expense of your loved ones!

Rascal even won an award! It was voted one of the Top 5 Applications in the MakeGamesWithUs iOS Development Competition. This is me presenting in front of a crowd of 200+ people :)
