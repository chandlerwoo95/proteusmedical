Our Mission

Proteus Medical's mission aims to deliver a universal device that will allow the user to effectively retrieve foreign bodies of various sizes. It will be adjusted to deliver convenience for the user without sacrificing the efficacy of the retrieval process, while simultaneously providing a robust structure to extend the lifetime of the device. The first application of the technology targets the most vulnerable patient segment: canines between the weight range of 40 and 60 lbs.
