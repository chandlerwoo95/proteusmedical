Our Technology

Proteus Medical is developing a simplified device leveraging our core technology, staying true to the philosophy that the best strategies for improving endoscopic foreign body retrieval should not alter current surgical practices. Veterinarians employ a number of methods to identify and obtain the foreign body, making it difficult to decide on a uniform device to retrieve an object. Because time and cost are important factors during the procedure, a more robust and uniform device will reduce the overall cost of the operation as well as reduce the learning standard, as many veterinarians will only learn through direct involvement with an endoscopic procedure. We've partnered with world-class physicians in both Boston and the University of Rochester Medical Center to validate our unique approach. Proteus Meidcal is wroking to provide an innovative combination of features that integrate seamlessly into the flow of operational care.
